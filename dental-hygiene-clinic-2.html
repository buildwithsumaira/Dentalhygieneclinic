FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Do you accept dental insurance? +
Yes! We accept most major dental insurance plans. We recommend calling us ahead of your visit to confirm your specific coverage. We also offer affordable self-pay options.
How often should I get a dental cleaning? +
Most patients benefit from a professional cleaning every 6 months. However, if you have gum disease or are prone to tartar buildup, we may recommend every 3–4 months for optimal health.
Is the clinic student-run? +
Our clinic is affiliated with NYC College of Technology, but all procedures are performed or supervised by fully licensed dental hygiene professionals. You receive the same quality of care as any private clinic — often at a fraction of the cost.
How do I book an appointment? +
You can book by calling us at (718) 260-5070 or by filling out the contact form on this page. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment time.
Is parking available near your location? +
Yes — street parking is available around Jay Street. We're also conveniently accessible by multiple subway lines (A, C, F, R at Jay St–MetroTech) making us easy to reach from anywhere in Brooklyn or Manhattan.
